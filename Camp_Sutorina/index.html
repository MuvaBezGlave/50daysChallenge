About us

Campsite is located in village Sutorina, city of Herceg Novi, Montenegro in a privately owned cultivated area with old trees and various tree cultures (such as oaks, ashs, elms) which together provide pleasant atmosphere for enjoyable stay.

The village is located 5 min from the cross border with Croatia (Debeli Brijeg) and 3 km from the Adriatic Sea in Igalo.
Camp facilities include parking, toilets, showers, electricity ,wi fi access. Campsite is ideally positioned in peaceful area, between beach (3 km) and regional road (200m).

Service plus - we offer parking spot and storage of Van, Trailer & Mobile Home on a monthly and annual basis. Price upon agreement. Near the campsite there is a gas station, a restaurant, shopping mall and hiking trails.


We are looking forward to welcome you.
